Where should I park?

What is there for my student?

How long is the service?

Gatherings last just over an hour. We usually start by singing then hear from a member of the teaching team. (Check out past sermons to get a sense of their teaching styles.)

What should I wear?

Most people dress casually, but you are welcome to wear whatever makes you comfortable.

Can my kids join me in the service?

In KidSpring, we teach kids about Jesus on their level in a safe, fun environment, but if you would prefer for your child to join your family in the service, they are welcome to do so!

Are there any other services the week of Easter?

Some campuses will have Good Friday services or other family friendly events the week of Easter. To learn more, subscribe to your campus’ Need to Know or visit your campus’ page to scroll through the events calendar.

Do I need to wear a mask?

Masks are no longer required at our campuses unless otherwise required by local ordinances. If you feel more comfortable wearing one, we encourage you to do so!
