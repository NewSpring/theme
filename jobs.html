Our Vision

We are a church that wants to see everyone everywhere in an everyday relationship with Jesus.

If that’s something you desire, too, come join our team!

In 2020 we started down a 10-year path we call Vision 2030. Until 2030, our vision is focused on three things: getting our current campuses in permanent homes, eliminating all of our debt (completed as of 2023), and planting ten churches that are not NewSpring campuses.

Hiring Process

God made you and uniquely gifted you.

Our hiring process is designed for discerning whether the role you’re applying for is the right fit right now.

We know your life and your time is valuable. So, if at any point in the process, it’s clear that the job you’re applying for isn’t the best fit, we’ll be in touch. You should also feel the freedom to email us at hiring@newspring.cc.
