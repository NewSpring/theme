Our Staff

Meet the Clemson staff

Jon Patterson

Campus Pastor

Jon grew up in the Clemson area and met Jesus at an early age. He went to college in Oklahoma, where he met the love of his life, Cady. After finishing seminary in 2018, Jon and Cady felt called back to the Clemson area, where they got connected to NewSpring Church. In 2020, Jon came on staff as the Discipleship Director for the Clemson Campus. He stepped into the campus pastor role in the fall of 2022. Jon and Cady are thrilled to do ministry in the Clemson area and are eager to see Jesus move in the hearts of the people here. They have one child, Callum, and another on the way!
